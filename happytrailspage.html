Problem Statement

My client came to me with a website that she had designed herself which she uses for her dog walking business. She ideally wants a website that represents her business to a more professional standard. She already has a Logo and colour scheme but she wants a more personal feel with visuals for example. I wanted to create a solution that would attract more clients and give her business a more approachable image.
